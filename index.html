Overview

The website provides various data visualizations related to migration within the European Union (EU). The EU has experienced significant population movement in recent years, with the influx of refugees and migrants from conflict zones in the Middle East and North Africa being a major driver of this trend.

The visualization about Acquisition of citizenship may be of interest to policy-makers, researchers, journalists, activists, advocacy groups, and the general public. It allow users to explore key indicators such as the number of citizenships granted, the countries of origin and the destination.
